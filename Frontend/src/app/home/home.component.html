With these two Icons, you can DELETE and EDIT a product.

In the Edit/Create section, I have made this interactive mobile phone to make the web a little prettier.
In it, you can visualize what you are typing in the form at real time. Clicking the cellphone speaker (at the top, the grey line), you can deactivate the screen and the lower button.
In the home button (black oval at the bottom), you can save changes. It has the same effect as clicking Guardar (Save).
